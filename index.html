Is Your School Truly Prepared?

Most school leaders feel confident in their safety plans—until a real crisis hits.

"Most schools feel unprepared for a real crisis because they lack practical, tested emergency response plans and staff who are truly trained to respond under pressure. Without expert guidance, schools remain vulnerable to preventable threats and unnecessary chaos during emergencies."

Learn From Nationally Recognized Experts

Our speakers bring real-world experience from the front lines of school safety

FBI Crisis Expert

Rob DePriest

National Security Consultant | Former FBI Crisis Response Leader

With over 20 years in federal law enforcement, Rob DePriest has led responses to some of the nation's most high-profile mass casualty events—including the Pulse Nightclub and Stoneman Douglas High School shootings. As the former head of the FBI Miami Crisis Response Program, Rob has trained with agencies worldwide to build scalable emergency plans for schools, stadiums, and government facilities.

"Rob's experience brings unmatched clarity to what schools must do now—not after a crisis."
De-escalation Expert

Bobby Brasher

DOJ-Certified De-escalation Instructor | Former Director of School Security

Bobby Brasher has trained educators, law enforcement, and international mission teams on how to de-escalate threats before they become tragedies. With deep roots in both law enforcement and Christian education, Bobby served as Director of School Security at a Texas boarding school before becoming a DOJ-certified De-escalation Instructor.

"Bobby's training turns fear and uncertainty into clear, calm action—exactly what school staff need most."
